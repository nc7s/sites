你来到了一片漆黑的原野。一片寂静中，你感觉有什么在蠢蠢欲动，但夜色笼罩一切。

眼睛渐渐适应了黑暗。左前方有点点微弱的亮光，看起来像是有人居住。

You step into a plain soaked in darkness. You feel, by instinct, something stirring in the depth of silence, but the night who cast the darkness stops your further inquiries.

Your eyes adapted to it. There is a patch of faint light. Looks like some kind of settlement.
